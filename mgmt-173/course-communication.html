 Course Communication Channels

Announcements

  Instructor Announcements

Instructor Announcements are sent out through your Canvas course by the instructor and TA(s) to keep you up to date on important information, such as upcoming assignments, changes in due dates, preparation work before a synchronous class session, etc. When an announcement is posted, Canvas will send you a notification based on your preferences. You can also find a list of all announcements posted in the class, in the left-hand navigation menu under Announcements.

PLEASE NOTE: Email messages generated by Canvas MAY be inadvertently filtered by your email service because the notifications contain images and links. To ensure that you receive your messages, you should check your junk email / spam folder or create a rule that sends all Canvas-related messages to a specific folder.

  Participation Points

To get started, you’ll need to activate your account so participation points earned in Yellowdig Engage are added to your grade in Canvas. You will earn participation points by posting, responding to others’ posts, receiving responses from other students, and receiving accolades for your posts. Be sure to tag your post with one or more “Topics”, to ensure your post is categorized and findable. Instructions for activation are available on the Pre-Course Assignments page.

  Group Collaboration

Overview of Groups

205 - Groups Overview from Canvas LMS on Canvas Studio.

Each group within Canvas has its own workspace, which includes a variety of tools and resources. When you're added to a group, the menu on the left side will include a new Groups icon. You can use this menu to access the groups in which you are included.

Your instructor might create an assignment, which is configured as a Group Assignment that only one member of the group needs to submit. Once a member of the group has submitted the assignment, then all members will be able to see that the assignment has been successfully submitted.
