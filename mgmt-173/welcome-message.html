Welcome to Business Intelligence for Analytical Decisions!

This course provides an overview of fundamentals of machine learning with a strong emphasis on business applications. It begins with an introduction to Artificial Intelligence and the AI ladder. It covers the basic techniques, operational considerations, and AI strategy. You will learn the fundamental building blocks of machine learning such as information theory, data visualization, structured data analysis: classification and clustering, unstructured data analysis and experiments.
