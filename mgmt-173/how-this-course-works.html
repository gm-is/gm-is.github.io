 Course Design

In this online course, you are expected to keep up with the weekly requirements and deadlines. 

  1. Weekly modules begin on Monday and end on Sunday.
  2. Work through modules SEQUENTIALLY. 
    The modules are designed to be completed in order. This means that you need to complete all the required items in each module before progressing to the next.
  3. It is required for you to be PRESENT in your online course.
    Your online presence and activity in this online course is an integral part of your learning journey. Your attendance (logging in regularly) and participation is required.
  4. Since this is an online course, the class will meet for OFFICE HOURS every week over Zoom. Attendance is not compulsory but recommended. If you can't make it for office hours, please use YellowDig or emails for asking questions. 
  5. If you have a doubt about something, you are stuck or just want to reach out, feel free to email me at vibs@uci.edu.

  Course Workload 

According to the University of California: “The value of a course in units shall be reckoned at the rate of one unit for three hours' work per week per term on the part of a student, or the equivalent.” This course has a workload that is approximately equivalent to a four-unit traditional course. Since this is a four-unit course, you are expected to do approximately 12 hours of work per week, for a total of 120 hours. 

In this course, you are expected to regularly participate and engage with the course by completing all assignments and tasks associated with each topical lesson, which includes watching all of the teaching videos, completing all the readings, assignments, discussions, etc.
