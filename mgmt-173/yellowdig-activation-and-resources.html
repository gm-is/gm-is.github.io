Activate

Yellowdig is a topic-based online discussion platform. It gives you and your classmates a space for ongoing course-related conversations. Since this course is fully online it is very important that you take the time to interact with your classmates, share your thoughts and experiences, ask and answer one another's questions, because engaging in discussion with one another enhances everyone's learning - most of all your own. 

When you participate in Yellowdig, you will receive points for interacting with your peers. You can earn points for posting and commenting, as well as receiving comments, reactions, and accolades on your posts. 

Yellowdig points are calculated weekly and reset at the end of every week (Sunday at 11:59 pm). You must participate each week in order to get participation points for that week. Please Note - Do not post ahead in future weekly discussions you will not earn the points during that week.

After you've activated Yellowdig, you can access the Yellowdig board via the Yellowdig Engage link on the left-hand navigation menu.

Activate Your Yellowdig Grade

  1. Before getting started, please activate your Yellowdig account to ensure your points earned push to the Canvas Gradebook
  2. You must enable and configure your grade with Yellowdig using a web browser (enabling and configuring your grade in Yellowdig using the Canvas and/or Yellowdig app is not available).
  3. After you have activated your Yellowdig account in a web browser, you may use the Canvas app on a mobile device by clicking the Yellowdig link in the course navigation menu.
  4. Click the "Activate Yellowdig" button below to activate your account.

How to Use Topics

DO NOT POST AHEAD of the current week. If you post in a future week you will not receive points for that week.

Look for the 'DISCUSS in Yellowdig' page in every module for that module's discussion prompt and associated topic tag.

Make sure to use the relevant topic tag on your posts. Tagged posts can easily be found by filtering, and help everyone's posts stay organized.

Please categorize your topics using the following prefixes:

  • COMMUNITY INFO:
  • BREAKING NEWS:
  • INTRODUCTION:
  • NEED HELP:
  • QUESTION:
  • AI AND ME:
  • CLASSIFICATION:
  • CLUSTERING:
  • AI IN SOCIETY:
  • AI IN BUSINESS:

  TIP: How to Maximize the Yellowdig Display

Depending upon the size of your device's screen, you may find that using Yellowdig embedded in Canvas is restrictive. If so, simply right-click on the hyperlinked name of the course's Yellowdig Community and then click "Open Link in New Tab" in the context menu to launch Yellowdig in its own browser tab. (This is my preferred way of using it.)
