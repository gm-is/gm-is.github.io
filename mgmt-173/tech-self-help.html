Module Item Completion (built in browser, hidden in app)

If you need technical support during the term, please follow each step below in the order you see listed.

STEP 1 | Please review the self-guided help documentation on this page if you experience technical difficulties during your course.

STEP 2 | Visit the HELP button located at the bottom of your Canvas Global Navigation (far left-hand side of your screen) and choose the support you need.

Canvas Global Navigation

STEP 3 | Visit the UCI's Learn Anywhere website at https://sites.uci.edu/learnanywhere/ for technical self-guided help.

STEP 4 | If you are unable to find a solution or require further technical assistance, please contact OIT for Support.

  • Login issues (WebAuth, DUO) : Contact the OIT Help Desk at 949-824-2222 or  oit@uci.edu.
  • Issues with connecting to campus services while off-campus:   You may need to use the OIT Software VPN . Contact the OIT Help Desk at 949-824-2222 or  oit@uci.edu  for assistance.
  • Using Canvas: Submit a support request ticket to the Merage Digital Learning team. Local help for these tools is available between 8am-5pm Monday-Friday. Outside of these hours, consider consulting the Canvas help student guides.
